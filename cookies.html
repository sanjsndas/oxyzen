🔐 Our Cookie Philosophy

🍪 What are cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences or actions. At Oxelra Flavor Circuit, we use only strictly necessary session cookies — these are essential for navigation, form functionality, and keeping the contact form operational. We never use advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or any persistent identifiers that follow you across the web.

🌶️ Your Privacy, Our Promise: Oxelra Flavor Circuit does not sell, share, or trade any personal data. The only information we store are the emails you voluntarily send via the contact form — and they are never combined with cookie data. We operate a strict no-tracking environment.

⚙️ Managing cookies & your choices

Because we only deploy essential session cookies, no explicit consent banner is required under GDPR/ePrivacy — these cookies are necessary for site operation. However, you can still control cookies via your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, but please note that blocking essential cookies may affect features like navigation or the contact form.

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Third-party cookies → Block or allow.
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage website data.
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions.

Even if you disable all cookies, you can still browse the flavor vault and read our red-hot recipes — only dynamic interactions like submitting inquiries might be impacted.
